# Privacy Policy — As You Wished

**Last updated: 2026-03-30**

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you play "As You Wished" (the "Game").

---

## Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal information is:

**Libertas Inc.**
Japan

Email: asyouwished@libertas-inc.co.jp

---

## Information We Collect

The Game may collect the following personal information, only if you choose to provide it:

- **Phone number** — Collected solely when you opt in to the phone call feature.

The phone call feature is entirely optional. You can play the full game without registering your phone number. Not registering will not affect the story branches — only some presentation effects will differ.

---

## Legal Basis for Processing (for EEA Users)

We process your phone number based on your **explicit consent** (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR). You may withdraw your consent at any time by deleting your phone number in the in-game Settings menu. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out prior to withdrawal.

---

## How We Use Your Information

Your phone number is used exclusively for:

- **In-game phone call effects** — The Game may place an actual phone call to your number as part of the gameplay experience.
- **SMS verification** — To confirm ownership of the phone number, a one-time verification code is sent via SMS.

Your phone number is never used for marketing, advertising, or any purpose other than the above.

Please note that receiving phone calls or SMS messages may incur charges depending on your carrier and plan.

---

## How We Store and Protect Your Information

- Your phone number is **encrypted using AES-256-GCM with securely managed encryption keys** before being stored.
- Your phone number is **automatically deleted after 72 hours** from the time of registration.
- You can **delete your phone number at any time** from the in-game Settings menu. Deletion from our systems is immediate.
- Your phone number is **not displayed during normal gameplay**. It may be temporarily visible only during explicit user actions (e.g., input or confirmation screens).

---

## Third-Party Services

We use **Twilio, Inc.** (a US-based company) to provide phone call and SMS verification services. When you register your phone number:

- Your phone number is sent to Twilio for SMS verification and phone call delivery.
- This constitutes a **transfer of personal data to the United States**.
- Data transfers to the United States are conducted based on appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), as implemented by Twilio.
- Twilio may retain call logs and SMS delivery records on their servers. We make best-effort attempts to delete Twilio call logs after each call completes, but complete erasure on Twilio's infrastructure is not guaranteed.

For more information, see the [Twilio Privacy Statement](https://www.twilio.com/en-us/legal/privacy).

---

## Countries Where Phone Features Are Unavailable

Phone call and SMS verification features are not available for the following country codes due to service limitations:

- **China (+86)** — Twilio does not support voice calls to mainland China.
- **Russia (+7)** — Service restrictions due to international sanctions.

Players in these regions can still play the Game in full without the phone call feature. The Game supports Chinese and Russian language options regardless of phone feature availability.

---

## Your Rights

### Right to Delete

You can delete your registered phone number at any time through the in-game Settings menu. Upon deletion:

- Your phone number is **removed from our active systems without undue delay**.
- Your registration token is invalidated.
- Twilio may retain related records subject to their own data retention policies (see above).

You may also request deletion by contacting us at the email address above.

### Right to Withdraw Consent

You may withdraw your consent to data processing at any time by deleting your phone number. This will not affect the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal.

### Right to Information

Your phone number is never returned or displayed by our systems after registration. The only information available to you is your registration status (registered, expired, or not registered) and the remaining time until automatic deletion.

---

## Data Retention

- **Phone number**: Automatically deleted 72 hours after registration via database TTL.
- **Server logs**: Retained for 30 days. We do not intentionally store phone numbers in application logs, and logging systems are configured to avoid capturing such data.
- **Twilio records**: Subject to Twilio's own data retention policies. See their privacy statement linked above.

---

## Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the minimum age required by applicable law in your jurisdiction). The phone number registration feature requires a valid phone number with SMS capability, which implies the user has access to a phone service.
